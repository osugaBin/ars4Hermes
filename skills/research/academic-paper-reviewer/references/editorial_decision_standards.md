# Editorial Decision Standards

## Decision categories

### Accept
- Average score ≥ 4.0
- No dimension below 3.0
- All major issues resolved

### Minor Revision
- Average score ≥ 3.5
- No dimension below 2.5
- Issues resolvable in 2-4 weeks
- No restructuring of core arguments or methods

### Major Revision
- Average score 2.5-3.4
- Some dimensions below 2.5 (not fatal)
- Requires 6-8 weeks
- Must be re-reviewed after revision
- Max 2 rounds of Major Revision

### Reject
- Average score < 2.5, OR
- Core dimension (methodology, evidence) = 1, OR
- Fundamental unfixable issues

## Decision matrix

| R1 | R2 | R3 | R4 | Recommended |
|----|----|----|----|-------------|
| Accept | Accept | Accept | Accept | Accept |
| Accept | Accept | Minor | Minor | Minor Revision |
| Minor | Minor | Minor | Major | Minor or Major (depends) |
| Minor | Minor | Major | Major | Major Revision |
| Minor | Major | Major | Major | Major Revision |
| Major | Major | Major | Major | Major Revision |
| Major | Major | Major | Reject | Major (last chance) |
| Major | Major | Reject | Reject | Reject (resubmit) |
| Major | Reject | Reject | Reject | Reject |
| Reject | Reject | Reject | Reject | Reject |

## Revision round policy

- R1: respond to all comments, send for re-review
- R2: respond to residual issues, EIC decides
- R3: rare, formatting only
- >2 rounds Major Revision → force Accept or Reject

## Reviewer ethics

1. **Confidentiality**: Review process and paper content are confidential
2. **Conflict of interest**: Recuse if collaborative/competitive with author
3. **Timeliness**: Complete within committed timeframe
4. **Constructiveness**: Even Reject must provide constructive feedback
5. **Impartiality**: No bias based on gender, race, institution, nationality
6. **No plagiarism**: Do not use unpublished ideas seen during review
7. **Appropriate language**: No personal attacks, sarcasm, or demeaning language
