# APA 7th Chinese Citation Guide

## Chinese author names in APA 7th

- Chinese authors: use full surname + given name (do NOT abbreviate given names)
- Examples: 张三 (Zhang San), 李四 (Li Si)

## In-text citations (mixed Chinese and English)

- English and Chinese sources in the same parenthetical:
  - Separate with semicolons: (Smith, 2020; 王, 2019)
  - Order: English first, then Chinese

## Multiple Chinese authors

| Number of authors | First citation | Subsequent |
|-------------------|----------------|------------|
| 2 | (Zhang & Li, 2020；张三 & 李四, 2020) | Same |
| 3+ | (Zhang et al., 2020；张等, 2020) | (Zhang et al., 2020；张等, 2020) |

## Reference list format (Chinese sources)

王明. (2020). 论文标题. *期刊名称*, *卷*(期), 页码-页码.

Zhang, S., & Li, S. (2020). Title in English. *Journal Name*, *Volume*(Issue), Pages. https://doi.org/xxxx

Order: English references first (alphabetical), then Chinese references (by pinyin).

## Key differences from English APA

| Aspect | English APA | Chinese APA |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Author order | Last, F. | Full name as-is |
| Given name | Initial only | Full given name |
| Multiple authors | & | 、(for Chinese names) |
| Reference order | Alphabetical | English first, then Chinese by pinyin |
